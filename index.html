Radial

Radar Chart Generator

Example | Project

  • What is this and what is it used for?

    This is a simple web-app that you can use to design what are radar chars, star plots, spider charts, or however you know them by! Radar charts are typically used for performance metrics in the professional world. Lately I've seen a rise in its use for traits, strengths and weaknesses, especially in character sheets.

  • Who is this for?

    This is for those who want a visual way of showing character traits or similar ideas, but aren't artists (or if you are an artist but don't need much control).

  • What do I do with it? How do I save the image?

    Whatever you want, be it saved onto a web page or directly pasted into a character sheet image. Simply, right-click on the chart and hit 'Save Image As...' or your browser equivalent. Note: As of this writing, this doesn't work for mobile-platforms.

  • Why don't I see any changes?

    A disadvantage to radar charts is that due to the geometry itself, at least two values need to be greater than zero. This doesn't work well where every value except one has no value.
